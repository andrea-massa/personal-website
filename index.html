About Me

My Story

As a first-generation immigrant and college graduate, I offer a distinctive perspective to my craft. Born in the beautiful city of Naples, Italy, and now residing in vibrant Charlotte, North Carolina, I have embraced the best of both worlds. Growing up in two distinct parts of the globe has enriched my understanding of diverse cultures and fueled my passion for technology.

Technology has always been a fundamental part of who I am. Starting with video-game modding, I delved into programming at 20, embarking on a captivating journey as a developer. Fueled by curiosity and a passion for innovation, each line of code I write opens up endless possibilities in the boundless realm of technology, constantly inspiring me.

In addition to software development, I have a range of interests that keep me engaged and inspired. I enjoy hiking, working out, swimming, food tasting, and learning about different cultures. Exploring these diverse passions helps me find balance and brings fresh perspectives to my work as a software developer.
