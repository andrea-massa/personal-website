My Story

I am a first generation immigrant and college graduate. Originally from Naples, Italy and now residing in Charlotte, North Carolina. I have had the blessing to grow up in two completely different parts of the world throughout my life.

I have been passionate about technology for as long as I can remember. It all started with video-game modding, until I started actually programming at the age of 19.

Some of my interests outside software development include: hiking, working-out, swimming, food tasting and learning about different cultures.
